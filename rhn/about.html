History

RentHub360 History RentHub360 was founded in 2025 by a team of entrepreneurs who recognized the need for a streamlined and efficient apartment rental platform in Nigeria. The company's mission is to provide a user-friendly and comprehensive online platform for individuals and families to find and rent apartments across Nigeria.

Key Milestones

- 2019: The platform expanded to other major cities in Nigeria, including Port Harcourt, Benin, and Ibadan.

- 2020: RentHub360 introduced a mobile app for iOS and Android, allowing users to search and book apartments on-the-go.

- 2022: The platform reached 10,000 registered users and 5,000 listed properties.

Mission and Values

RentHub360 is committed to providing a seamless and enjoyable apartment rental experience for its users. The company's values include:

- Customer satisfaction: Providing a user-friendly platform that meets the needs of renters and landlords.

- Innovation: Continuously improving and expanding the platform to meet the evolving needs of the market.

- Integrity: Ensuring transparency and honesty in all dealings with users and partners.
